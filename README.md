**Customer Cohort & Retention Analysis**
**Project Overview**
This project focuses on analyzing customer behavior over time using cohort and retention analysis techniques. The analysis helps in understanding customer engagement patterns, retention trends, and customer acquisition performance.

The project was developed using SQL, and Power BI to perform data cleaning, transformation, analysis, and visualization.

**Objectives**
Analyze customer retention trends over time
Identify high-performing and low-performing customer cohorts
Track monthly customer acquisition patterns
Build an interactive dashboard for business insights

**Tools & Technologies**
SQL – Data transformation, cohort analysis, and retention calculations
Power BI – Dashboard creation and data visualization

**Dashboard Pages**
**Page 1: Cohort Analysis Overview**
Cohort retention heatmap
Retention performance across different customer groups
Identification of customer engagement patterns over time
**Page 2: Retention & Acquisition Trends**
Retention trend analysis using line charts
Monthly customer acquisition analysis using bar charts
Business growth and customer activity insights

**Key Insights**
Customer retention decreases gradually over time
Certain customer cohorts show stronger retention performance
Monthly acquisition trends highlight fluctuations in customer growth
Retention and acquisition analysis provide valuable business insights
